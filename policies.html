Financing

DHP accepts all major credit cards including Visa, MasterCard, American Express and Discover. In addition we also accept payment via PayPal. Payments made via PayPal must come from a verified account with a confirmed shipping address.

Delivery

Products are shipped within 1-2 business days from when the order is placed, pending payment authorization and verification.

Return Policies

Item(s) purchased from DHP must be returned at the customers expense in new, unused condition with original tags still attached in order to receive a refund, within 30 days of receiving the shipment.
